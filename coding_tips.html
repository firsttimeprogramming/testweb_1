如何找到.ext 的文件并把它复制到某个文件夹

—————————————

find . -name "*.ext" > list.txt

cat list.txt | awk 'BEGIN {a="'"'"'"}{print "cp "a$0a" Directory"}' > script.sh

sh script.sh
